### 1. Financial Simulation & Stochastic Processes
* **Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM):** Simulated random price paths for financial assets.
* **Volatility Analysis:** Computed historical volatility and covariance matrices.
* **Risk Metrics:** Calculated **Value-at-Risk (VaR)** to estimate potential portfolio losses.

### 2. Derivative Pricing (Options)
* **Monte Carlo Pricing:** Implemented algorithms to price **European Call and Put options**.
* **Black-Scholes Model:** Compared simulation results with the theoretical Black-Scholes formula.
* **Variance Reduction:** Used **Antithetic Sampling** to improve simulation accuracy and speed.

### 3. Information Theory in Finance
